National Ministries Allocation Data Form

Instructions

Please use this form to report your parish's financial information for the National Ministries Allocation Process. The due date for completing this form is May 15, {{ year - 1 }}. Please bear in mind that the form must be reviewed and approved by the parish priest, president, treasurer, and internal audit committee chairperson by that date.

Financial data should be reported on a calendar-year basis for {{ year - 3 }} and {{ year - 2 }}, and must include any indirect support received by the parish from organizations or individuals. The Archdiocese Finance Committee and Archdiocese Legal Committee have approved the following text to clarify how and when to report indirect support, as required under the Archdiocese Regulations:

All expenses incurred and paid by a supporting foundation, private foundation, public charity, an individual, or any other entity: (i) on behalf of a parish, (ii) are required to operate a parish, or (iii) or maintain parish related assets, are required to be included on Line B of the Archdiocese Allocation Form. The Archdiocese Finance Committee shall have the discretion to work with a parish to impute expenses to a parish and such discretion shall be binding.

You may share this form by sending the complete URL to another person. Please note that the URL contains a randomized key that allows the recipient to view the form and modify its contents.

In order to edit the form, please click the pencil button in the top-right corner. It should turn green to indicate you are editing the form. Only one person will be able to edit the form at a time. Your changes will be automatically saved as you type, as long as there is a working internet connection. A red error message will appear if the connection is lost or the data is not being saved.

In order to save you time, we have pre-populated the form with information that was submitted last year. Please correct any information that has changed and delete any information that applied only to {{ year - 4 }}.

Verify Data Connection

Please follow these steps to verify that this form is working correctly on your web browser:

  1. Click the pencil button to enter editing mode (it should turn green).
  2. Make a small change to one of the fields in the form.
  3. Refresh the page in your browser.
  4. Verify your changes are still visible. If so, that means the form is working correctly and your changes are being saved.

If the form does not seem to be working, try using a newer web browser, such as Firefox or Chrome. You may also contact Geoffrey Gowan for assistance at geoffrey.gowan@gmail.com or (510) 230-7650.

If the form is working correctly on your browser but your internet connection is temporarily lost, a red error message will appear and the data entry fields will become locked until the connection can be restored.

C9. Fundraising Expenses

Please report deductible fundraising expenses below, in accordance with the following guidelines:

  • Expenses for fundraising events such as festivals, dinner dances, community dinners, and golf outings are deductible. However, fundraising events to benefit a Greek Dance program are specifically excluded from being deductible.
  • Only expenses that are directly attributable to the fundraising activity are deductible. Do not allocate a portion of other parish expenses to the fundraising activity, such as the priest's salary or facility expenses.
  • Expenses associated with ongoing parish ministries or activities are not deductible, such as facility rental, catering, stewardship, fellowship, bookstore, candle, or koliva expenses.
  • Utility expenses for fundraising events may be deducted only if they were actually measured apart from regular parish usage.
  • As with all deductions, this deduction only applies for amounts that were included in parish gross expenses (section B, above)
