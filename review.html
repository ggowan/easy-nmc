C9. Fundraising Expenses

Please report deductible fundraising expenses below, in accordance with the following guidelines:

  • The expenses must have been directly caused by a fundraising activity, such that if the fundraising activity had not been undertaken, the expenses would not have been incurred.
  • Expenses associated with the regular parish stewardship program are not deductible.
  • Do not deduct a portion of regular parish expenses such as the priest's salary or facility maintenance as fundraising expenses.
  • Expenses associated with rental property or businesses operated in parish facilities should not be considered part of the parish and should be excluded from parish gross expenses. However, in order to be excluded, such expenses must actually be measured separately from other parish expenses.
  • As with all deductions, this deduction only applies for amounts that were included in parish gross expenses (section B, above).
  • Utility expenses for fundraising events may be deducted only if they were actually measured apart from regular parish usage.

Expenses that are typically deducted in this section cover food, materials, rental expenses, and special labor for events such as festivals, dinner dances, and golf outings.
